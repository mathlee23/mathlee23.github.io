# Welcome to my blog!

> *"A mathematician who loves the precision of numbers and the depth of classic stories."*

> *Research Interests : 
>  * **Primary Focus:** Transcendental Number Theory, specifically exploring the deep connections between Schanuel’s and Gelfond’s conjectures. $N$-Exponentials Conjecture, Infinite Power Tower, Constant Problem 
>  * **Special Functions & Combinatorics:** Investigations into degenerate Bell, Fubini, and Bernoulli polynomials.
>  * **Interdisciplinary:** The intersection of rigorous mathematical logic and the lyrical beauty found in Romanticism literature and visual arts.*

Out of Offices: Sights, Sounds, and Thoughts.
