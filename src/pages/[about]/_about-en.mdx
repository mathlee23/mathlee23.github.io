# About Me

## Hyunseok Lee

> *"My reason for being here in this world is Mathematics, and that reason itself is My World."*

I am a mathematician specializing in transcendental number theory. I earned my Ph.D. studying the relevance of weak Schanuel's conjecture and Gelfond's conjecture in CRAS 1934, and I currently lecture at Kwangwoon University. I have published over 30 papers in international journals on topics including special polynomials and combinatorial identities.

> **Contact:** luciasconstant@gmail.com

- **Supervisor:** Taekyun Kim

**2014 – 2019**

- **Master's Thesis:** *On Transcendental Numbers*
- **Supervisor:** Haseo Ki
