# About Me

## Hyunseok Lee

> *"Beyond the equations: Exploring the beauty of mathematics, arts, and life."*

I am a mathematician specializing in transcendental number theory. I earned my Ph.D. studying the relevance of weak Schanuel's conjecture and Gelfond's conjecture in CRAS 1934, and I currently visting professor at Kwangwoon University. I have published over 30 papers in international journals on topics including special polynomials, combinatorial identities and transcendental number theory.

> **Contact:** luciasconstant@gmail.com

> **Research Interests** : 
>  * **Primary Focus:** Transcendental Number Theory, specifically exploring the deep connections between Schanuel’s and Gelfond’s conjectures. $N$-Exponentials Conjecture, Infinite Power Tower, Constant Problem 
>  * **Special Functions & Combinatorics:** Investigations into degenerate Bell, Fubini, and Bernoulli polynomials.
>  * **Interdisciplinary:** The intersection of rigorous mathematical logic and the lyrical beauty found in Romanticism literature and visual arts.
