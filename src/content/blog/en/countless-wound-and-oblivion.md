There is a profound and subtle landscape that only winter can offer. The beauty of this season’s scenery is a completely different kind of beauty altogether. Somehow, winter holds an inexplicable warmth. The warmth exuded by the landscape, the warmth of waiting for the approaching spring. At the boundary where evening falls, the subtly painted borders of the sky, those overlapping colors—those are the hues that only nature can capture. I have always loved them, and I will continue to love them.


No matter how far technology advances or if the era of AI fully arrives, I believe we will weather the harsh tides of any industrial revolution. That is, as long as we do not lose our intrinsic value as humans, and as long as we strive to understand, cherish, and love one another more deeply.


Someone will keep singing, and someone will keep painting. Even if it is a song no one hears or a painting no one sees, someone will steadily create and silently raise their voice. And I believe that will become a piece of history, a page in a well-worn book. I dream of a world where people can love humanely, exactly as humans should.


Even if it is but a vain hope, I want to embrace it, endure with it, and live my life holding onto it. Until the day I return to the sky.


Yes, I will simply walk the path given to me with all my might. Whether it is a smooth, well-paved road, a rough trail full of gravel, a muddy path, or a winding, rain-soaked uphill climb, it does not matter. Even if my feet blister, even if I grow weary and lose my way with every step, I will simply keep walking silently at my own pace. That is the goal and the promise of my life.
